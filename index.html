What Is AppBlueprint?

AppBlueprintTM is a tool for anyone with an app idea. It allows you to document and plan every little detail your app will need. Gone are the days of writing your idea down in Notes and looking back a week later saying "what was I thinking"? Plan how it looks, how it acts, target your audience, monetization, marketing strategies and so much more.

Capture Every Detail

Fill out each section of the app to create your AppBlueprintTM, which you can hand to a team member, manager or development team. From there you can collaborate, get approval, an opinion or just keep it in your pocket for later.



Any Time Any Where

AppBlueprintTM is all about allowing you to quickly keep track of your next million dollar idea, on the go. We have broken it out into categories of data points to allow easy documenting and brainstorming no matter where you are or time of day.

Application Features

Quick Add

Quickly add your app idea with it's most basic requirements. A name and description.

Determine App Platforms

Will your app be made for iOS, Android and a Web App? Toggle on or off the platforms your app will need.

Demographic Targets

Determining your target audience is important. Choose demographic targets like gender, location, age and profession.

Look & Feel

Do you want a modern looking app? How about defining some adjectives that come to mind - or if you need some help get inspired with a provided list.

The Back End

Does your app need hosting? Is there a specific library you want to use? How about data storage like MySQL, CoreData and more.

Communication

Do you need to interact or communicate with your user base? Will you use emails? How about some text-messages.

Monetization

How do you plan on making money? Is this a free app with in-app purchases? Or is there an up front cost?

Marketing

Does your app need a website? How about a Facebook page or Twitter account?

Your Team

Who will bring this idea to life? Is it just you or specific team members.

Notes

A space to get all your thoughts out there with an empty notes area.

Elevator Pitch

Plan out your elevator pitch so when you need to give it to someone, you are all buttoned up.
