What Is AppBlueprint?

AppBlueprintTM is a tool for anyone with an app idea. It allows you to document and plan every little detail your app will need. Gone are the days of writing your idea down in Notes and looking back a week later saying "what was I thinking"? Plan how it looks, how it acts, target your audience, monetization, marketing strategies and so much more.

Capture Every Detail

Fill out each section of the app to create your AppBlueprintTM, which you can hand to a team member, manager or development team. From there you can collaborate, get approval, an opinion or just keep it in your pocket for later.
